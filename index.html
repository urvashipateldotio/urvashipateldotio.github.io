About Me

I'm a Senior Integration Developer with over 14 years of experience building enterprise integrations with Boomi, EDI, and APIs. I've helped organizations connect their systems, automate workflows, and make data flow seamlessly.

Now I'm learning Python and AI engineering in public. I believe the best way to learn is to teach, so I document my journey, share my struggles, and build projects that help others learn too.

In 2024, I founded Integration Insider, a knowledge hub for integration developers. It started as my way of organizing what I know, and it's growing into a community resource.

When I'm not coding or writing, you'll find me experimenting with AI tools, reading about developer experience, or thinking about how to make technical concepts more accessible.
